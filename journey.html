What have I learned & accomplished so far?

I have gained so much knowledge in the last 14 weeks I don't think I could write it all down. When I think back about the first week and our homework it's a massive difference. I rememebr being in the library thinking "wow this is so hard I will never know how this works". that was JUST HTML/CSS. Now we are neck deep in the back end and working toward front end proficiency. It's like night and day and when I am feeling very frustrated It gives me a lot of perspective to look back to that first study group.

My biggest accomplishment is becoming a better learner. I haven't been in school for a long time but I know that this time around I am learning so much more. I am more dedicated and diligent about this program and it is so rewarding. I have gotten better at networking, working as a group and asking questions because of this course and I know it is only up from here.

Let's look back at the first week...

Before the first week of class even started I already wanted to quit. I started the prework and I was already lost. I was frustrated and confused and had myself convinced I wasn't cut out for this. I started from scratch, turned up my concentration and pushed thru to the first day.

Moving on to the very first week. Cue second full on breakdown. Nearly in tears in the library because I felt defeated by basic HTML and CSS. I thought - "this is the easiest part of the class and I can't even get out of the gate on it!". Once the breakthough happened it was like an addiction began. Creating something then working every line to make sure it's perfect becomes so rewarding that you don't want to stop. (Or you can't stop, depending on the project)
