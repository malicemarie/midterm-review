My goals for the remainder of the course:

For the remainder of this course I want to focus on the details and honing my skills. I still feel like I have to copy and paste a lot and It's partly because I am struggling with my syntax and a better understanding of the details will help me a lot. Less copy and paste, more coding!

What will I accomplish before the course completes; and after.

Before the course completes my main goal is to do a mock interview. I am terrified and I am so nervous to participate so It's part being prepared and a BIG part getting up the guts. After the course is over I want to work really hard to make connections and find genuine interest in different companies

In order to accomplish these goals I was to become more prepared for interviews. Not just by making more connections and deciding what direction I want to go but also being technically prepared. Putting in extra work to learn data structures, algorithms & syntax that is important to know off the top of my head.

How is my path evolving?

When I started boot camp my main goal was to go back to school and change my career. I had no idea what I was going to be good at, what I was going to like and dislike, and how I was going to become employable in a new field. As class moves along I have been so overwhelmed with information that I am even MORE excited about the direction this may go. There are so many branches of tech and I can't wait to continue to learn and grow in and outside of class so I can find my place.
